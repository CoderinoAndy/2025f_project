About

This dashboard organizes emails into four views (All Emails, Response needed, Read only, Junk Confirmation), and lets you expand an email to view full contents, generate summaries, draft replies, revise, send, and move messages to junk.

How to use
  • All Emails: shows everything by recency, with type and priority (stars).
  • Response needed: emails that require a reply.
  • Read only: informational emails that donâ€™t require a reply.
  • Junk Confirmation: uncertain junk mail that needs user confirmation.
  • Click any email row to open the expanded email view.
